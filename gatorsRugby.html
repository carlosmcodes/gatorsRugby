Values

The San Francisco State University Rugby Football Club adopts the same values as World Rugby and promotes these values in the team as well as in each other. They are the following:

INTEGRITY: Integrity is central to the fabric of the game and is generated through honesty and fair play.

PASSION: Rugby people have a passionate enthusiasm for the game. Rugby generates excitement, emotional attachment and a sense of belonging to the global rugby family.

SOLIDARITY: Rugby provides a unifying spirit that leads to life-long friendships, camaraderie, teamwork and loyalty which transcends cultural, geographic, political and religious differences.

DISCIPLINE: Discipline is an integral part of the game both on and off the field and is reflected through adherence to the laws, the regulations and rugbyâ€™s core values.

RESPECT: Respect for team-mates, opponents, match officials and those involved in the game is paramount.

Mission

To promote and teach the sport of rugby, developing player athletes to their highest potential in a positive environment while encouraging discipline, commitment, integrity and honor both on and off the pitch.

To encourage each player to experience and enjoy the culture of rugby, and to have FUN in playing the sport.

To inspire in our players a strong work ethic, where discipline, integrity, honesty, humility and passion combine into strength of character.

To aspire to the very highest standards of play.
